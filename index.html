Apple's history begins with the founding of the company by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in 197612. They worked in Jobs' garage to create their first personal computer, the Apple I2. This company then produced other computers such as the Apple II, Apple III, and Lisa. The famous Apple logo with a bite on an apple also has its own story. Apple continues to release innovative products such as the iMac, iPod, iPhone and iPad.
Apple has again scored a new achievement. The company started by Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak had reached a market capitalization value of up to 3 trillion US dollars or more than IDR 42,000 trillion (exchange rate IDR 14,280) in the first trading session in 2022, to be precise Monday (3/1/2022). This figure makes Apple again break a new record as the first company in the world to successfully reach a market capitalization value of up to 3 trillion US dollars.
Apple is well known among gadget lovers. A company that always provides the latest breakthroughs in the world of technology. Apple never recedes from mass media coverage. It could be said that Apple is the best brand at the moment.
